Book to Film Comparison

Romeo & Juliet to Romeo + Juliet (1996)

The Book: Romeo and Juliet

Romeo and Juliet is a tragic play written by William Shakespeare. It tells the story of two young lovers from feuding families whose relationship ends in heartbreak. The play explores love, fate, family conflict, and sacrifice, and it remains one of the most famous works in English literature.

The Movie: Romeo + Juliet (1996)

Romeo + Juliet, directed by Baz Luhrmann, is a modern film adaptation of Shakespeare’s play. It keeps much of the original dialogue but places the story in a contemporary setting with dramatic visuals, fast editing, and a memorable soundtrack. The movie presents the same tragic love story in a bold and stylish way.
