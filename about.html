About This Archive

This archive explores the relationship between literature and film. Many classic books have been adapted into movies, bringing stories to life for new audiences.

The goal of this project is to showcase famous books alongside their film adaptations and compare how stories are told across different mediums.

From Shakespeare’s tragic love stories to Jane Austen’s romantic classics, this archive highlights some of the most influential adaptations in cinema.
