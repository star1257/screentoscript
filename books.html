Books & Film Adaptations

Romeo and Juliet

  • Original play by William Shakespeare (1597)
    • Romeo and Juliet (1968) – directed by Franco Zeffirelli
    • Romeo + Juliet (1996) – directed by Baz Luhrmann
    • Gnomeo & Juliet (2011) – animated adaptation
    • West Side Story (1961) – inspired musical
    • Romeo Must Die (2000) – loose action adaptation

Pride and Prejudice

  • Novel by Jane Austen (1813)
    • Pride and Prejudice (1940) – starring Greer Garson
    • Pride and Prejudice (1995) – BBC miniseries with Colin Firth
    • Pride & Prejudice (2005) – directed by Joe Wright
    • Bride and Prejudice (2004) – Bollywood musical
    • Pride and Prejudice and Zombies (2016) – horror parody

The Great Gatsby

  • Novel by F. Scott Fitzgerald (1925)
    • The Great Gatsby (1926) – silent film (lost)
    • The Great Gatsby (1949) – starring Alan Ladd
    • The Great Gatsby (1974) – with Robert Redford
    • The Great Gatsby (2000) – TV movie
    • The Great Gatsby (2013) – directed by Baz Luhrmann

The Winter's Tale

  • Play by William Shakespeare (1623)
    • The Winter's Tale (1910) – silent short
    • The Winter's Tale (1967) – TV adaptation
    • The Winter's Tale (1981) – BBC Shakespeare series
    • The Winter's Tale (1999) – Royal Shakespeare Company recording
    • The Winter's Tale (2014) – stage adaptation film

Much Ado About Nothing

  • Play by William Shakespeare (1623)
    • Much Ado About Nothing (1993) – directed by Kenneth Branagh
    • Much Ado About Nothing (2005) – TV adaptation
    • Much Ado About Nothing (2011) – filmed stage performance
    • Much Ado About Nothing (2012) – Joss Whedon's modern version

A Midsummer Night's Dream

  • Play by William Shakespeare (1600)
    • A Midsummer Night's Dream (1935) – starring James Cagney
    • A Midsummer Night's Dream (1968) – directed by Peter Hall
    • A Midsummer Night's Dream (1999) – with Kevin Kline
    • The Woods (2015) – modern reimagining
    • A Midsummer Night's Dream (2017) – BBC adaptation

Twelfth Night

  • Play by William Shakespeare (1623)
    • Twelfth Night (1937) – early TV version
    • Twelfth Night (1980) – BBC Television Shakespeare
    • Twelfth Night (1996) – directed by Trevor Nunn
    • She's the Man (2006) – modern adaptation set in a high school
