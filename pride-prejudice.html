Book to Film Comparison

Pride & Prejudice to Pride & Prejudice (2005)

The Book: Pride and Prejudice

Pride and Prejudice is a novel by Jane Austen that follows the relationship between Elizabeth Bennet and Mr Darcy. The book explores themes of love, class, marriage, reputation, and personal growth. Austen combines romance with sharp social observation, making it one of the most widely read novels in English literature.

The Movie: Pride & Prejudice (2005)

Pride & Prejudice (2005), directed by Joe Wright, is a film adaptation of Austen’s novel. It captures the emotional tension between Elizabeth and Darcy while presenting the story through beautiful cinematography, period costume, and strong performances. The film stays close to the novel while adding a cinematic style.
