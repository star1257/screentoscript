The Great Gatsby to The Great Gatsby (2013)

The Book: The Great Gatsby

F. Scott Fitzgerald’s The Great Gatsby explores wealth, longing, illusion, and the American Dream through the mysterious figure of Jay Gatsby and his obsession with Daisy Buchanan.

The Movie: The Great Gatsby (2013)

Directed by Baz Luhrmann, the 2013 adaptation brings Gatsby’s world to life with lavish visuals, dramatic energy, and a modern soundtrack while preserving the story’s tragedy and glamour.
