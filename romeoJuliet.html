Book to Film Comparison

Romeo & Juliet to Romeo + Juliet (1996)

The Book: Romeo and Juliet

Romeo and Juliet is a tragic play written by William Shakespeare. It tells the story of two young lovers from feuding families whose relationship ends in heartbreak, making it one of literature’s most famous love stories.

The Movie: Romeo + Juliet (1996)

Directed by Baz Luhrmann, Romeo + Juliet places Shakespeare’s language into a vivid modern setting. It keeps the emotional core of the original while using bold visuals, music, and fast-paced style.
