Romeo & Juliet to Romeo + Juliet (1996)

The Book: Romeo and Juliet

Romeo and Juliet is a tragic play written by William Shakespeare. It tells the story of two young lovers from feuding families whose relationship ends in heartbreak.

The Movie: Romeo + Juliet (1996)

Romeo + Juliet, directed by Baz Luhrmann, is a modern film adaptation of Shakespeare’s play with a dramatic modern setting and strong visuals.
